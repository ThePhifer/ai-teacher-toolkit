Automated Assessment Tools

Gradescope Automates grading for handwritten, typed, and multiple-choice assignments using AI-powered handwriting recognition and rubric-based scoring, complete with detailed analytics and flagging for human review. CoGrader A free, rubric-based AI essay grader that imports custom or standards-aligned rubrics to instantly assess essays and generate personalized feedback closely aligned with expert human grading. EssayGrader.ai Provides consistent, rubric-based scoring for essays at scale, evaluating coherence, prompt adherence, grammar, and spelling through an advanced NLP engine with instant results. GradeWrite.AI Auto-checks essay submissions for word count, AI-generated content, and rubric adherence, flagging discrepancies early and enabling teachers to focus on high-value, personalized feedback. Writable Employs a human-in-the-loop model with ChatGPT to draft comments and preliminary grades for student writing; teachers review and finalize, ensuring meaningful feedback at scale. EssayCheck.ai Delivers rapid essay scoring (under 90 seconds) with detailed insights on structure, style, and alignment to prompts, ideal for quick turnaround on writing assignments.
