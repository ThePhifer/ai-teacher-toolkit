Automated Assessment

A reference guide to AI-powered tools for assessments

GradeCam Enables teachers to create, capture, and grade assessments by scanning student responses on paper or online; offers real-time scoring, detailed item analysis, and reporting.
MagicSchool.ai Generates and grades customized multiple-choice quizzes aligned to readings or standards, offering instant scoring and detailed question analytics for formative assessment.
Gradescope Automates grading for handwritten, typed, and multiple-choice assignments using AI-powered handwriting recognition and rubric-based scoring, complete with detailed analytics and flagging for human review.
CoGrader A free, rubric-based AI essay grader that imports custom or standards-aligned rubrics to instantly assess essays and generate personalized feedback closely aligned with expert human grading.
EssayGrader.ai Provides consistent, rubric-based scoring for essays at scale, evaluating coherence, prompt adherence, grammar, and spelling through an advanced NLP engine with instant results.
GradeWrite.AI Auto-checks essay submissions for word count, AI-generated content, and rubric adherence, flagging discrepancies early and enabling teachers to focus on high-value, personalized feedback.
Writable Employs a human-in-the-loop model with ChatGPT to draft comments and preliminary grades for student writing; teachers review and finalize, ensuring meaningful feedback at scale.
EssayCheck.ai Delivers rapid essay scoring (under 90 seconds) with detailed insights on structure, style, and alignment to prompts, ideal for quick turnaround on writing assignments.
ZipGrade A smartphone-based multiple-choice grading app that scans answer sheets via camera and provides instant scores, item analysis reports, and class performance insights.
TeachShare Quickly builds diagnostic, formative, or summative multiple-choice assessments aligned to standards or skills, complete with answer keys and auto-scoring features.
Musely A free online platform supporting multiple question types (MC, T/F, short answer, essay) with automatic grading and a dashboard for performance analytics and reporting.
